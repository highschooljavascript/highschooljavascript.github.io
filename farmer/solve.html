Welcome to the Farmer, Wolf, Goat, and Cabbage Problem
A farmer and his wolf, goat, and cabbage come to the edge of a river they wish to cross. There is a boat at the river's edge that only the farmer can row. The farmer can take at most one other object besides himself on a crossing, but if the wolf is ever left with the goat, the wolf will eat the goat; similarly, if the goat is left with the cabbage, the goat will eat the cabbage. Devise a sequence of crossings of the river so that all four characters arrive safely on the other side.
